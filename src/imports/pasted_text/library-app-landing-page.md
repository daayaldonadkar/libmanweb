You are a world-class product designer and conversion-focused UX expert.

Design a PREMIUM, MODERN, and HIGH-CONVERTING mobile-first landing page for a SaaS app made for Indian library owners.

IMPORTANT:
This is NOT a generic landing page. It must feel real, trustworthy, and premium (like Razorpay, Notion, Stripe). Avoid anything that looks fake, template-like, or low-quality.

---

🧠 PRODUCT CONTEXT:

The app helps small to mid-size Indian library owners manage:

* Student members
* Monthly/weekly plans
* Payment tracking (cash + UPI)
* Defaulters (who haven’t paid)
* Seat management (General seating + Private cabins)
* WhatsApp payment reminders

Target users:

* Non-tech-savvy library owners in India
* Currently using registers or WhatsApp
* Care about saving time and not losing money

---

🎯 DESIGN GOAL:

Make the website so clear and trustworthy that a library owner instantly understands:

* “This is made for me”
* “This will save me money”
* “This is easy to use”
* “I should try this right now”

---

🎨 VISUAL STYLE:

* Premium but simple (Razorpay / Apple / Notion style)
* Clean white or soft off-white background
* Primary color: deep blue / indigo (trust)
* Soft shadows, glassmorphism (light, not heavy)
* Rounded corners, spacious layout
* High-quality typography (modern SaaS style)
* Mobile-first layout (VERY IMPORTANT)
* Avoid clutter and avoid too many colors

---

🏗️ LANDING PAGE STRUCTURE (STRICT):

Design ALL sections below:

---

1. HERO SECTION (Above the fold)

* Headline:
  “Stop Losing Money on Missed Library Fees”

* Subheadline:
  “Track payments, manage seats, and send WhatsApp reminders — all from your phone.”

* CTA buttons:

  * Primary: “Start Free”
  * Secondary: “Chat on WhatsApp”

* Visual:
  Realistic mobile mockup showing:

  * “Pending: ₹4,500”
  * “Collected Today: ₹2,000”
  * Clean dashboard UI

* Trust badge:
  “Made for India 🇮🇳 | Secure Data”

---

2. PROBLEM SECTION

* Show relatable pain:

  * Messy registers
  * Missed payments
  * Seat confusion
  * Time wasted

* Use visual comparison:
  ❌ Old way (register)
  ✅ Smart way (app UI)

---

3. SOLUTION SECTION

* Show clean app interface
* Emphasize simplicity:

  * “See who hasn’t paid”
  * “Track every rupee”
  * “No confusion”

---

4. HOW IT WORKS (VERY IMPORTANT)

* 3-step visual flow:

  1. Add your library
  2. Add students
  3. Start tracking payments

* Add:
  “Setup takes less than 5 minutes”

---

5. FEATURES (BENEFIT-FOCUSED CARDS)

Design modern feature cards with icons + screenshots:

* Never miss a payment again
* Send WhatsApp reminders instantly
* Visual seat management (General + Cabin)
* Track daily income (₹)
* See defaulters instantly

---

6. SOCIAL PROOF

* Testimonial cards (realistic Indian names)

* Example:
  “I recovered ₹3,000 in one week just by tracking defaulters.”

---

7. PRICING SECTION

* Headline:
  “Costs less than one student”

* Show:

  * Free plan (limited seats)
  * Paid plan (₹299/month)

  “Recover just 1 missed fee = app pays for itself”

---

8. FAQ SECTION

* Simple accordion UI
* Questions:

  * Do I need a computer?
  * What about cash payments?
  * Is my data safe?

---

9. FINAL CTA SECTION

* Headline:
  “Start managing your library the smart way”

* CTA:
  “Start Free” + “Chat on WhatsApp”

---

⚡ CONVERSION ELEMENTS (MUST INCLUDE):

* Sticky bottom mobile CTA bar (“Start Free”)
* Floating WhatsApp button (bottom-right)
* Show money values (₹) in UI
* Use simple English (no technical jargon)
* Focus on clarity over decoration
